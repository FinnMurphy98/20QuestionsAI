Welcome to 20questionsAI!

This website allows you to play games of 20 Questions against ChatGPT.

Just login, or sign up if you don't have an account, and you can start playing right away!

In 20 Questions, the answerer thinks of a person, place or thing, and the questioner has to guess what it is with 20 questions or less.

Each question can only be answered with a yes or no by the answerer, so pick clever questions to narrow down the possibilities.

Before each game, you can choose to play as the answerer or questioner.

ChatGPT is primed and ready to play from the start, so all you need to do is start messaging.

Once you feel the game has come to a resolution, you can finish by submitting the game result.

You can view your statistics in your personal home page, such as your overall and role-specific win rates.

You can see a list of all your previous games in the history page, and click them to view the message history.
